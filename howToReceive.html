1. Wait for the Product to Reach Your House

You can do anything during this time.

2. Make sure the product is left at your house.

If you are at home, you make kindly collect the product in person. If you are outside your house, the postman will call you on the registered number on your account to decide wether to leave the package at your doorstep or to deliver it another day.

3. Open the Shipping Box

We advise to do this by using scissors or a knife to cut the tape.

4. Enjoy your product!

Spend time with your product, and read/watch it to help you learn or practice a foreign language!

5. Leave a review!

If you have time and patience, kindly leave a review on our website (product's page) to tell other people how much you liked the product, or how much you hated it. And then if you have even more time, kindly review our website and our company's efficiency in supplying your needs.
